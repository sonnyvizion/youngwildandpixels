Mercure

Mercure’s brand identity and website were entirely crafted in-house — from creative direction to digital experience. As a premium iPhone case brand, the challenge was to create a universe that feels sleek, modern, and timeless. The design embraces minimalism, sharp typography, and a restrained color palette, allowing the product to take center stage while reinforcing a strong, confident identity.

The website, built in Webflow, delivers a smooth and fluid experience where form and function align. Subtle interactions, precise layouts, and a clean visual flow create a sense of quality at every scroll. No excess, no distraction — just design that feels as refined as the product itself. Because premium is a feeling, not a label.
