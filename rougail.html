Le Rougail
Restaurant

Le Rougail’s visual identity and website were crafted from the ground up — creative direction, logo, custom illustrations, and Webflow build. The goal was to translate the restaurant’s soul into a bold yet welcoming digital experience. Colors, shapes, and typography were carefully chosen to reflect warmth, authenticity, and personality, while keeping the interface clear and easy to navigate.

The result is a vibrant identity that feels both modern and rooted. Playful illustrations meet a structured layout, creating a balance between character and clarity. A brand that feels alive on screen, just like it does on the plate. Because when design tells a story, you can almost taste it.
